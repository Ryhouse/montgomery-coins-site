Common Questions

How much does an appraisal cost?

Nothing. Verbal appraisals are always free, whether you sell to us or not.

Should I clean my coins first?

Please don't. Cleaning permanently damages coins and can cut their value dramatically. Bring them exactly as you found them.

I inherited a collection and have no idea what's in it. Can you help?

That's most of what we do. We'll walk through it piece by piece and tell you what's common, what's collectible, and what it's worth.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome. For estates or large collections, call ahead so we can give it the time it deserves.
